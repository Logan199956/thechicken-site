🌍 What is the Chicken Universe?

The Chicken Universe is a highly advanced civilisation where chickens secretly run the world. Humans believe they are in control… but chickens are watching.

🐔 The Great Chicken Origin

According to ancient egg-scrolls, chickens evolved from the legendary “Feather Titans” approximately 8,000 years ago in the Valley of Snacks.

🥚 Core Beliefs

  • All eggs must be respected
  • Spawning chickens is sacred
  • The sacred cluck echoes through time

🏛️ Chicken Government

The Chicken Council is ruled by the Supreme Hen known as Eggatha Prime. Decisions are made through competitive pecking tournaments.

⚠️ Human Warning

If you hear clucking at 3AM… it’s already too late.
